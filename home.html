HOME

A Dog's Life is like no other dog boarding and doggy daycare facility in the Chicago area! Each day is filled with fun, friendship, and stimulation to ensure that dogs of all shapes, sizes, ages, and breeds are happy, exercised, and entertained. Whether you’re headed out on the Eastside or downtown Chicago for the day or you’re off on a two-week Alaskan cruise, your dog will receive the best care possible at A Dog's Life center.

Dog Boarding

After a full day of play and social activity, our boarding guests retire to climate-controlled rooms
with glass fronts, off-the-floor beds, heated floors, and soft music.

Dog Daycare

Whether you’re off to work or headed to downtown Chicago,
at the end of the day your dog will be tired, happy, and ready to go home with mom or dad.

Dog Grooming

Your dog will return home relaxed, clean, and happy when you use any of our dog grooming,
bathing, and health services.

Dog Training

All dogs love to learn and want to please you. Our comprehensive dog training includes one-on-one sessions, group classes, and a Board & Train program.
