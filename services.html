Dog Boarding

Luxury Dog Boarding in Chicago, IL

A Dog's Life Resort is happy to offer all dogs boarding with us their own personal, cozy room with full glass-front, kept cool by a climate-controlled facility and warmed with radiant-floor heating. Each room comes complete with a comfortable bed and soft, clean bedding, plus 24/7 access to clean water – our rooms are also plenty big enough to accommodate most bedding from home if your companion has a favorite blanket or bed. If you are interested in boarding multiple dogs, we can certainly place them in the same room if they get along well, two large dogs or up to four small dogs may comfortably share one of our suites together. A Dog's Life also offers Double-Rooms, featuring two rooms with an adjoining door for families with multiple dogs who can be together sometimes, but would benefit from their own space at mealtime and overnight.

Dog Daycare

Safe, Fun & Healthy Dog Daycare at A Dog's Life Center

At A Dog's Life Center socialized dogs can get the physical and mental stimulation that they need to have a happy and healthy daycare experience! At ADLC we focus on the whole dog. Your pooch will enjoy small playgroups matched with similar playstyles, comfortable rest periods, cuddles, positive reinforcement guidance, and daily report cards including photos.

The traditional free-for-all daycare style often results in overly tired or exhausted pups. Exhaustion breeds behavior issues and can lead to many dogs not enjoying the experience. Mental stimulation is just as important as physical activity. Our goal with ADLC is to engage every dog physically AND mentally while also providing adequate and necessary rest periods.

Dog Grooming

Professional Dog Grooming in Chicago, IL

It is A Dog's Life's utmost desire to always do what is best for the dog in any of our services and our grooming salon strives to make each pet’s experience a positive one. Doggy Haven’s experienced and knowledgeable grooming staff offers an array of services including bathing, haircuts, nail trimming or grinding, ear cleaning and/or plucking, anal gland expression, and teeth brushing. All of our grooming services require an appointment. Pets must be fully vaccinated with all required immunizations. We ask that you bring the vaccine info with you to your appointment, or send it to us ahead of time, as we are not able to contact your veterinarian for you. Puppies must be at least 8 weeks old, and can be up to 6 months old without a Rabies vaccination.

Please let our groomers know about all health and/or behavior concerns your dog might have: lumps, bumps, scratches, scrapes, previous surgeries/injuries, arthritis, breathing issues, heart conditions, disabilities, anxieties, places your dog doesn’t like to be touched, things your dog might react negatively to. In addition, we use a variety of shampoos to best suit your dog’s coat and skin needs. Please let us know if your dog has allergies, sensitive skin or coat issues that you would like us to address.

Pricing

Grooming prices depend on size, age, comfort, coat condition, and the type of service being done for each dog. We can give an estimated cost over the phone, but this is not a guaranteed price. A more accurate quote can be given upon meeting the dog.
